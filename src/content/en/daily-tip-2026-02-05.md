### Warm Up Your Morning, Doston!Kya haal hai, sab log? February ka mahina chal raha hai aur sardi abhi bhi acchi khaasi hai, hai na? Is mausam mein kai baar subah uthne ka mann nahi karta aur thodi susti si lagti hai. Ayurveda kehta hai ki yeh Kapha dosha ke badhne ke karan hota hai. Par chinta mat karo, mere paas ek simple, garam-garam nuskha hai jo aapki subah ko ekdum fresh bana dega!### Why This Simple Drink RocksJab bahar thand hoti hai, hamari body bhi andar se thodi slow ho jaati hai. Hamari digestive fire, yaani Agni, bhi thodi kamzor pad jaati hai. Yeh garam-garam drink aapki Agni ko jagayega, Kapha ko balance karega aur aapko din bhar ke liye energy dega. Socho, ekdum fresh start!### How to Make Your Morning MagicYeh banana toh itna aasan hai ki aap hans padoge!*   **Garam Pani:** Ek glass gunguna ya garam paani lo. Thanda paani bilkul nahi!*   **Adrak (Ginger):** Ek chhota tukda adrak ko kaddukas kar lo ya koot lo aur paani mein mila do. Adrak garam hoti hai aur digestion ke liye best hai.*   **Shahad (Honey):** Jab paani thoda thanda ho jaaye (peene layak), tab ek chammach shuddh shahad mila do. Garam paani mein shahad mat daalna, uske gun kam ho jaate hain.*   **Nimbu (Lemon):** Thoda sa nimbu ka ras bhi nichod sakte ho, agar aapko acidity ki problem nahi hai. Yeh detox mein help karta hai.### Best Time to SipSubah uthte hi, brush karne ke baad, khaali pet isko peeyo. Dekhna, aapka pet bhi saaf hoga aur poora din halka-halka feel hoga.### Ek Chhoti Si Tip AurIske saath, thodi der walk kar lo ya kuch light stretching kar lo. Thoda paseena niklega toh aur bhi accha hai. Aur breakfast mein bhi kuch halka-phulka hi khaana.Toh kya kehte ho, aaj se shuru kar rahe ho na yeh garam-garam subah ka nuskha? Try karo aur mujhe batao kaisa laga! Stay healthy, stay happy!
